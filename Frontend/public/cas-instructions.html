CAS PORTAL · AY 2025-26

Application Filing Instructions

MIT Art, Design and Technology University, Pune

1. Before You Begin

  • Keep scanned PDFs of all certificates ready (UG, PG, PhD, joining letters, FDP certificates).
  • Upload each proof to your institutional Google Drive and obtain a shareable link ("Anyone with the link → Viewer").
  • Have your Google Scholar, Scopus, ORCID and Vidwan IDs handy.
  • If not applicable, in Patents, Research Projects, and PhD Supervision forms, click Add Entry and Save Draft without entering any details.

2. Filling the 9 Sections

The dashboard contains nine sections. All nine must be filled (at least one valid entry where applicable) before the Submit Application button is enabled.

#SectionWhat to provide
1Personal ProfileEmployee ID, qualifications, joining dates, research IDs
2Previous ExperienceAll institutions worked at prior to MIT-ADT
3Administrative RolesCommittees, coordinator roles, conveners
4Teaching WorkloadCourses taught in AY 2025-26 with hours
5FDP / STTP / MOOCProgrammes attended ≥ 1 week
6PublicationsJournals, conferences, book chapters with DOI
7PatentsFiled / Published / Granted
8Research ProjectsFunded projects with role and amount
9PhD SupervisionScholars under supervision/co-supervision

3. Date Format

All dates must be entered in DD/MM/YYYY format using the calendar picker. For non-applicable dates, don't enter the data and enter NA in drive link text field.

4. Auto-Calculated Fields

  • Total Teaching Experience — calculated from your first Asst. Prof joining date to today.
  • Post-PG Experience — calculated from your PG completion date to today.

5. Submission Workflow

  1. Save each section as draft — the system auto-saves on every keystroke.
  2. Use Validate & Save on each section to confirm completeness.
  3. When all 9 sections show ✓, the Preview & Submit button activates on the dashboard.
  4. Review the consolidated preview, then click Submit Application.
  5. Submitted applications cannot be edited and become visible to the Admin panel.
